Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Our team is available 24/7.

How do I choose the right lawyer for my case?
+
During your free initial consultation, we assess your case and match you with the most suitable attorney based on practice area, case complexity, and your personal needs.
What is domestic violence from a legal perspective?
+
Domestic violence encompasses physical, emotional, financial, and sexual abuse within intimate partner or family relationships. Legally it carries serious criminal penalties and our family law experts can help you navigate protection orders and your rights.
How is child custody determined in court?
+
Courts prioritize the best interests of the child, considering factors like parental stability, the child's relationship with each parent, living arrangements, and the child's own wishes for older children. Our family attorneys guide you through every stage.
Are your lawyers licensed to practice internationally?
+
Yes. With offices in the US, UK, EU (Zurich), and UAE (Abu Dhabi), SUEZY has licensed attorneys in each jurisdiction. For cross-border matters we coordinate a multi-jurisdictional team to ensure seamless representation.
What are your fees and billing structure?
+
We offer transparent billing — hourly rates, fixed-fee arrangements, and contingency options depending on case type. All fees are discussed upfront. Your first consultation is always free.
